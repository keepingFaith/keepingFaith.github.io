Projects

Hands Out

Hands Out reaches out to the homeless in Tempe, Arizona. We grab pizza, blankets, Bibles, dog treats, water bottles, and candy and go out to love on people. These people have stories to tell, and when they are given the time of day, they open up beautifully.

If interested, we do this on the third Friday of each month.

The Future Movement

Suicide has touched so many in our community. It is a darkness that not many can openly talk about. The Future Movement takes the initiative to let people in our community know that they are loved and that suicide is not the answer. We go out with signs and stand near exit ramps by our local freeway.

If interested, we do this on the first Monday of the month.

Adopt A Block

Every Saturday morning, we "Adopt A Block". There is always work to be done in our neighborhoods and always people in need who can use a helping hand. It's a great way to make connections with our community and let them know they matter.

If interested, we do this every Saturday morning!
