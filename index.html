In The Beginning

Hey guys! The name is Faith!

A little about myself: I'm twenty-two years old. I attend 29:11 Church in Arizona and am actively seeking Christ's presence in my life. I've heard some pretty negative things about the Millennial Generation. They are said to be lazy, entitled and Godless.

To be honest, some of it is true. There are a lot of lazy and Godless Millennials. But I happen to know plenty of hard-working and God fearing millennials as well. And if you don't know any, then you are not hanging with the right people. The group of Millennial’s I'm talking about are passionate about God and eager to serve their community. They pray for one another and use words of encouragement to build one another up. They seek wisdom and work hard.

I want to show the world what a millennial’s walk with Christ looks like. This is one of the platforms I use to accomplish that. I have Devotionals available, volunteer projects and blog posts, so take a look and see if anything peeks your interest!
